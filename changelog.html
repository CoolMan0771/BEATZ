Changelog

Version 5

Full Screen now available for every song

Full screen is now available on songs that don't have videos.

Other notes

- Added an animation when hovering over Grid/List View and Play/Shuffle buttons

Version 4.5

Play and Shuffle

There is now an option on the top left of the screen where you can either play all songs in order or shuffle all.

Rewind and Skip

There is also 2 new options in the player bar where you can restart the current song or if you have something in queue, then skip the song. If there is nothing in queue, the skip button will not show.

Other notes

- The GUI in fullscreen mode will now show when the mouse moves anywhere

- When GUI in fullscreen mode dissapears, the cursor will also dissapear

Version 4.0

Design is Expanding

The header and navigation bar gets the design and the navigation stays put.

Grid or List?

You can now change the view of the songs by just pressing the buttons on the top right of the page. You can switch from grid to list view.

Other notes

- Changed the thickness of dividers

Version 3.1

Other notes

- Whenever going fullscreen, the GUI will pop up no matter where the mouse is

Version 3.0

Queueing!!

Added a right click menu that will give the option to play the song you choose next or add to queue. At the end of a song, it will then play what is next in queue.

Other notes

- Removed "other features are coming soon" in song info

- Changed the color of the player to match the audio seeker

Version 2.04

Updated GUI

We've updated the arrangement of the full screen and there's going to be something that will replace that orange box.

Updated GUI

We have changed the fullscreen GUI to look more like that glassy style like before. It goes away when your cursor isn't on it and it reappears when you do.

Other notes

- Changed the down arrow image for the original player to be more rounded like its fullscreen button counterpart.

Version 2.0

New Full Screen Option

There's a new full screen option to see the full video when playing a track. There you can find song information and a play and pause button. The full screen option will only appear on tracks that have videos in the background.

New GUI Style

In the fullscreen option you can find a new user Interface style. It is somewhat transparent to the current video and has more of a fluid design.
